Bio

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Bo An. Previously I spent a wonderful year as a research assistant in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Pingzhong Tang. Prior to that, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2016.

My research interest lies in robust deep learning, including robustness against noisy labels, out-of-distribution examples, and adversarial examples. The goal of my research is to develop reliable learning systems, which can work safely and adaptively in the presence of noisy/biased/malicious data stream in either the train or test datasets. Research topics that I am currently working on include:

  • Learning with (open-set) noisy labels;
  • Out-of-distribution detection;
  • Adversarial attack and defense;
  • Uncertainty estimation.
